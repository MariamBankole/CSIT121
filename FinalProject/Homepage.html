Welcome to My Caricature World

I'm a caricature artist who creates humorous and exaggerated depictions of people. Let me show you my unique style!

who's the artist?

My name is Mariam, and I've been drawing caricatures for over 5 years. I specialize in creating funny and whimsical portraits that capture the essence of the subject.

My unique style is inspired by cartoons, and I love to exaggerate facial features to create a hilarious caricature that will make people laugh.

Services

I'm available to draw live caricatures at your event. It's a great way to entertain your guests and give them a fun memento to take home.
