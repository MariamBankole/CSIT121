Wizkid

Wizkid is a Nigerian singer and songwriter who rose to fame with his debut album "Superstar" in 2011. He has since released several hit songs and collaborated with many international artists.

I am a big fan of Wizkid's music and love his unique sound that blends Afrobeats, reggae, and hip-hop.
