Coconut Joys

You'll love these no-bake,no-fuss sweets

Ingredients
  • Butter
  • Shredded Coconut
  • Pecan
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate
Directions
  1. In a large bowl, combine the coconut, confectioners' sugar and butter. Form into 1-in. balls
  2. Using the end of a wooden spoon handle, make an indentation in the center of each ball. Fill with chocolate. Sprinkle with pecans. Place on a waxed paper-lined baking sheet. Chill until chocolate is firm. Store in the refrigerator.

Coconut joys are my favorite recipe because they're a delicious, sweet and crunchy treat that is perfect for satisfying a sweet tooth. The rich and decadent flavor is hard to resist.

Additionally, they are quick and easy to make.
